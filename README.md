# Music Store Sales Analysis — SQL

## Overview
Analysed a digital music store database to identify revenue trends,
top-spending regions, and highest-performing music genres using
advanced SQL queries.

## Tools & Techniques
- SQL — CTEs, JOINs, Window Functions, Aggregations
- Database: Chinook (Digital Music Store)

## Business Questions Answered
- Which country generated the most revenue?
- Which music genres drive the highest sales?
- Who are the top 5 highest-spending customers?

## Key Findings
• A few cities generate the majority of revenue, making them ideal for marketing campaigns or music festivals.
• Rock genre appears frequently among listeners, showing its strong popularity in the dataset.
• Some artists dominate the track counts, showing higher production and listener demand.
• A small group of loyal customers contributes a large portion of revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention.
• Music preferences vary by country and customer, which helps businesses create targeted marketing strategies.

## Skills Demonstrated
Advanced SQL querying, data wrangling, pattern recognition,
business insight generation
